🖨️ How to Print at Home

  1. Open the PDF size that matches your printer paper.
  2. In your print settings, select "Actual Size" or "Scale to 100%" (Do not select "Fit to Page" as it may distort the margins).
  3. You can print the entire workbook at once, or just print the Daily Pages one week at a time if you prefer!

📱 How to Use Digitally (iPad / Tablet)

  1. Save the PDF to your tablet.
  2. Open your favorite PDF annotation app (like GoodNotes, Notability, Xodo, or Noteshelf).
  3. Import the PDF into the app.
  4. You can now write directly on the pages using your stylus or Apple Pencil!
