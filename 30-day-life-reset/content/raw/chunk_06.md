### Day 23: Your Values

**Task 1:** Write a fast list of 10 things you care about. No editing — whatever surfaces first. Take two minutes.

**Task 3:** For each circled value, finish the sentence: "This matters to me because ___." One sentence each.
**Quick Win:** Read your circled values out loud. Notice if any of them surprise you.
**Why It Works:** Named values act as a decision filter — they make future choices feel clearer and less draining over time.
