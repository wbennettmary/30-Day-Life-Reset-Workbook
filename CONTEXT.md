# Project Context — 30-Day Life Reset Workbook

## PRODUCT
- Name: 30-Day Life Reset Workbook
- Tagline: Small shifts. Real momentum. Your reset starts here.
- One-liner: A gentle 30-day system that turns overwhelm into clarity through daily actions that actually stick.
- Launch price: $5.99 (first 7 days) → Standard: $8.99
- Platforms: Etsy + Gumroad
- Target page count: 85-95 pages total
- Formats: US Letter (8.5x11) + A4 (210x297mm)

## TARGET AUDIENCE
- Persona: The Overwhelmed Restarter
- Demographics: Women 28-45, college-educated, working professionals or parents
- Pain: Stuck in autopilot, overwhelmed, disconnected from personal goals
- Fear: Rigid systems, failing again, adding more pressure
- Want: Control, clarity, sustainable habits without guilt
- Behavior: Buys self-help content, rarely finishes it

## 4-WEEK FRAMEWORK
- Week 1 (Days 1-7): Foundation — Energy & Awareness
- Week 2 (Days 8-14): Momentum — Habits & Productivity
- Week 3 (Days 15-21): Growth — Body, Mind, Environment
- Week 4 (Days 22-30): Integration — Identity, Values, Future

## BEHAVIORAL PACING (critical)
- Days 1-10: Small wins + emotional recalibration ONLY. No big goals.
- Days 11-20: Introduce structured habits. Max 1 new habit category per week.
- Days 21-30: Identity-level reflection + future planning.
- Motivation touchpoints REQUIRED at: Day 1, 3, 10, 17, 24, 30
- Missed-day reset language REQUIRED at: Day 8, 15, 22

## CSV CONSTRAINTS (for Canva Bulk Create)
- Headers are LOCKED: Day_Number,Title,Goal,Task_1,Task_2,Task_3,Quick_Win,Why_It_Works,Reflection,Mood_Scale,Tomorrow_Focus
- Max 200 chars per field
- NO line breaks inside any field
- Commas inside fields: wrap entire field in double quotes
- Mood_Scale: fixed literal in Canva — do NOT bind to CSV column
- Encoding: UTF-8 — verify in plain text editor, never Excel

## TONE RULES
### MUST USE
- "Let's" / "You've got this" / "Small wins add up" / "Progress, not perfection"

### MUST AVOID (banned words)
- transform, journey, unlock, level up, optimize, hack, dominate, crush
- supercharge, ultimate, overnight, spiritual, religious framing
- Perfectionist or guilt framing
- Tasks that actually take more than 10 minutes

### VOICE
- Calm knowledgeable friend — not a coach, not a guru
- Emotional arc: Calm reassurance → Gentle momentum → Quiet confidence
- Max 2-line paragraphs
- Simple language — no psychobabble
- Gender-neutral, no income/lifestyle assumptions
