Check In With Yourself

01
Foundation
Today's
goal Notice how you actually feel right now — without trying to fix anything.
  • 1
    Task one · 10 min
    Set a 5-minute timer and sit quietly without your phone. Notice thoughts and body tension.
  • 2
    Task two · 10 min
    Write 3 words that describe how you've been feeling lately. No editing — just what's true.
  • 3
    Task three · 10 min
    Write one word for how you want to feel by the end of these 30 days. Put it somewhere visible.
Quick win · 1–2 min
Take 3 slow breaths before you do anything else today.
Why it works
Naming emotions reduces their intensity — a well-documented effect called affect labeling.
