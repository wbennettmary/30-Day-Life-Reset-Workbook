You don't need to overhaul your life. You just need somewhere to start.

The 30-Day Life Reset Workbook is a guided daily planner for anyone who feels stuck, burned out, or like they've been going through the motions for too long — and is ready for something to actually change.

No rigid systems. No guilt. Just one clear step each day.

---

✦ WHAT'S INSIDE

• 66 beautifully designed pages
• 30 guided daily pages — each with a clear goal, 3 tasks (10 minutes each), a quick win, a reflection prompt, a mood check, and a tomorrow focus
• 4 weekly checkpoints to track your progress and reset if needed
• 7 specialty tracker pages: habits, mood, health, focus, home, finances, relationships
• 3 bonus sections: 50 Tiny Habits list, 7-Day Emergency Reset, 90-Day Roadmap

---

✦ HOW IT'S STRUCTURED

Week 1 (Days 1–7): Foundation — energy and awareness
Week 2 (Days 8–14): Momentum — habits and productivity
Week 3 (Days 15–21): Growth — body, mind, environment
Week 4 (Days 22–30): Integration — identity, values, future

Each week builds gently on the last. Week 1 is deliberately light — it's about noticing, not doing. By Week 4 you'll have enough quiet momentum that the bigger questions feel approachable rather than overwhelming.

---

✦ WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT

Most planners assume you're already organized. This one starts where you actually are.

✔ Behavioral pacing — designed so early days rebuild energy, not drain it
✔ ADHD-friendly layout: one focus at a time, open checkboxes, generous white space
✔ Built-in missed-day reset so a skipped day never means starting over
✔ No hustle culture language — just steady, sustainable progress

---

✦ WHAT YOU RECEIVE

→ Instant digital download — no waiting
→ Two PDF files: US Letter (8.5 × 11 in) + A4 (210 × 297 mm)
→ Print at home or at a local print shop
→ Works with GoodNotes, Notability, and most tablet PDF apps

---

✦ HOW TO GET STARTED

1. Download both files — use whichever size fits your printer
2. Print the full workbook or just the pages you need
3. Complete the 4-page setup section (takes under 10 minutes)
4. Turn to Day 1
